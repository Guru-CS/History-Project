What is Quinine?

Quinine is a medication used to treat malaria.

Normally Taken

Quinine was mixed with alcohol, like whiskey or gin, and was commonly used by soldiers daily during the Civil War.

Where is it Derived From?

Quinine came from the bark of the cinchona tree, native to the Andes in South America
